Refund Policy

Any Problems with Our Services?

Refund Eligibility

If you are not satisfied with our service, you may be eligible for a refund. Refunds are granted in the following cases:

  • Service Not as Described: If the service you received substantially differs from what was promised.
  • Technical Issues: In cases where technical issues prevent you from accessing or using our service, and these issues cannot be resolved by our support team within a reasonable timeframe.
  • Pro Cancellation within Refund Period: If you decide to cancel your Pro subscription within the specified refund period, you may be eligible for a refund.

Refund Process

To request a refund, please contact our customer support team with your order details. We will review your request and process the refund within 7-10 business days.
